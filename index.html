About

My business is very family oriented. I started building decks with my dad when I was 9 years old. After I graduated from high school, him and I framed houses together for 2 years, during this time I also built new construction decks.

In 2016 I began building decks for my Brothers' company exclusively: Deck and Drive Solutions. In 2018, I became his main subcontractor, and I started running crews. In 2020, I decided to branch out and start my own decking business, Custom Decks LLC.
